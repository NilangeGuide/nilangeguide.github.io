Nilangé Stars

Nilangé publishes a series of general guides to cities, states of India and awards exceptional quality of establishment/work with stars. The Nilangé Guide awards three stars (Gold, Platinum & Diamond) based on the quality of experience. A fourth star, Ultimate is awarded to the best establishment/work with unbeatable perfection. The reviewers are anonymous and stars are awarded based on multiple factors that are specific to each field. An establishment can even lose a star when the set standard is failed to attain in the following years of operation.
